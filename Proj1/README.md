# 📊 Sales Data  Analysis Dashboard (Power BI)

## 📌 Project Overview

This Power BI dashboard analyzes the **Sales Data** from a sales dataset. It provides interactive visualizations to understand sales performance by month, year, product, order status, vehicle category, and state.

The dashboard helps identify sales trends, customer demand, and product performance for better business decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze total quantity ordered.
- Track monthly sales trends.
- Compare yearly sales performance.
- Identify top-selling products.
- Analyze order status distribution.
- View sales by vehicle category.
- Compare quantity ordered across different states.

## 📈 Dashboard Visualizations

### 1️⃣ Monthly Quantity Ordered
Shows the monthly trend of total quantity ordered.

### 2️⃣ Product-wise Entry Count
Displays the number of entries for each Product Code.

### 3️⃣ Year-wise Quantity Ordered
Compares total quantity ordered across different years.

### 4️⃣ Order Status Distribution
Shows percentage of orders by status:
- Shipped
- Resolved
- On Hold
- In Process
- Cancelled
- Disputed

### 5️⃣ Vehicle-wise Quantity Ordered
Displays quantity ordered for different vehicle categories:
- Classic Cars
- Vintage Cars
- Motorcycles
- Trucks & Buses
- Planes
- Ships
- Trains

### 6️⃣ State-wise Quantity Ordered
Shows quantity ordered across different states/countries.

## 📚 Key Insights

- Highest sales were recorded in **November**.
- December experienced a significant decline.
- Most orders were successfully **Shipped (93%)**.
- Classic Cars contributed the highest quantity ordered.
- California generated the highest quantity ordered among all states.
